\begin{abstract}
  This article provides an overview of various known methods for representing a
  finite lattice as the congruence lattice of a finite algebra or proving that
  such a representation exists. Using these and a recently discovered method, we
  show, for all but one lattice, that every lattice with at most seven elements
  is isomorphic to the congruence lattice of a finite algebra.  
\end{abstract}
